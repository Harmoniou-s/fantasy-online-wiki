Troubleshooting:

In this section I’m going to address very basic problems people encountered. If you just started using the PixelShop 3k and encountered a problem, please have a look in this section before posting on the forum.

Q: My brush is not painting!
A: Either you didn’t create a layer yet or you’re drawing on a layer that is overlapped by another layer. Make sure you select the layer you really want draw on!

Q: When I look at my creation in the “Animation” window/ on the web it is not transparent, it’s just white!
A: You didn’t paint your alpha channel yet. Use the alpha tool to do it! You should probably also read the section “About the Alpha Channel”
