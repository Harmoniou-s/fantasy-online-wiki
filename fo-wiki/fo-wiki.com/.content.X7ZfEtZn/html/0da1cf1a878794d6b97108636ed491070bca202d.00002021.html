Questions and Answers, Part 2
28. What do AGI/INT/STR/STA do? Where should I put my stat points?

One point in Agility (AGI)gives you +2 Armor and +0.07 Critical %, some Weapons also require Agility to equip, to get 100% Critical you need 1330 Agility. One point in Intellect (INT) gives you +15 Maximum MP and increases your MP recovery rate, some Spells also require Intellect to use. One point in Strength gives you +1 ATK Power, some Weapons also require Strength to equip.One point in Stamina (STA)gives you +10 Maximum HP. Some Armors also require Stamina to equip.
Put your points into STR for a warrior, AGI for an archer and INT for a mage.

30. Are there any in-game commands?

Yes, there are several. Just type them in the chatbox and press enter.


/w [name] [message] --- This will whisper to the person with your message. Noone will be able to read it but them.
/t [name] [message] --- This will whisper to the person with your message. Noone will be able to read it but them.
/g [message] --- This will talk so everyone can read it.
/online --- This will tell you how many players are online.
/pos --- Tells your position on the map in the form of coordinates.
Shift + R --- Whispers back to the last person who whispered to you.

31. What is the Rest System?

The rest system is a feature that simulates player exhaustion. This is because it is very hard to fight evil monsters every day. So you need to rest.
There are three different states: "Rested", "Tired" and "Exhausted".
You start with 2 x xp gain when you are "Rested".
At a certain point you will get tired and it will go down to 1 x xp gain when you are "Tired".
Then it becomes 1/2 x xp gain when you are "Exhausted".

When you rest you will get either 10K rested xp or the current level's xp requirement as rested xp.

Every 3 hours (on relog/login) you will be given 1/8 of the total current rest possible, with a full refresh at 24 hours.
